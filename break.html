Taking a Break

Hi. I'm Benjamin.

Due to some recent events, my mental health has taken a significant hit. During this, I've been focusing on dedicating more of my free time to programming, which has actually ended up damaging my mental health further.

I feel like I have to be contributing to everything and can't just take a break.
Well, I'm quite tired of it at this point.

In an attempt to focus on myself and my mental health, I'll be taking a break from the internet for a currently undecided amount of time. It might be a few weeks, it might be a month, I don't know.

Services

All of the websites I host (see my statuspage for a list) will stay online and functional. Nothing will change here, everything will still be updated and maintained as needed behind the scenes.

Accounts

All of my online accounts (GitHub, YouTube, Roblox, etc.) will remain dormant until I come back. I have no plans to interact with anybody on any of them, nor do I have plans to upload anything.

Contact

An exception to all these previous no-internet rules is my contact information.
In case you need to contact me during my break, you can still send me mail at ben@iipython.dev.
